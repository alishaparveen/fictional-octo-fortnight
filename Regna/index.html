Our Aim & Mission

Our aim is to build "affordable pre-fabricated net zero solar powered houses" making India's future energy secure. We strive to develop technology to build a solar-powered, sustainable house for the Indian middle class, which could serve as the answer to India's growing energy and housing problems. Our mission is to bring about a change in the Indian building industry, by demonstrating the affordability of sustainable housing, to formulate and document a design process, which could be replicated to create sustainable homes in a resource efficient way and to raise awareness in the nation about energy efficiency, responsible energy use and the potential of renewable technology with emphasis on solar energy.

Interactive Events

To raise awareness on green building design & renewable energy, talks, lectures,workshops and conferences are conducted involving students, academicians & professionals in various sectors of the society.

Talks / Lectures

Four talks/lectures were conducted, the latest being a lecture on Design Elements of a Net- Positive Energy House - A Case Study of Team SHUNYA to bring an understanding among people on how to design a Net-Positive energy house.

School & College Workshops

Four workshops were conducted across various schools and colleges in Mumbai, for kids aged 10-13. The focus of these talks were sustainability and energy efficiency in homes to educate the children about simple ways to implement energy savings.

Conferences:

Given that industry is quite a key target for communications, we have participated in about 5 conferences across the country where we have presented our project in the form of a ppt, a talk or a video and attended some conferences as a part of internal skill development.
